About pain

Pain, also known as Nagato, is a central antagonist in the Naruto series.He is the leader of the Akatsuki organization and possesses the Rinnegan, a powerful dojutsu that grants him various abilities.Pain's philosophy revolves around achieving peace through pain and suffering, believing that true understanding can only be attained through shared agony. His character is complex, as he seeks to end the cycle of hatred in the ninja world, albeit through extreme methods.
